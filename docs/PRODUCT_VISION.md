# Product Vision - Job Finder Agent

## Overview
Job Finder Agent is a comprehensive, cross-platform (web and mobile) career automation platform designed to assist professionals throughout their job search, professional development, and interview processes. By leveraging advanced artificial intelligence, the platform automates tedious career management tasks, provides personalized guidance, and empowers users to present their best professional selves.

## Key Features & Modules

### 1. Resume Builder
- Dynamic resume builder utilizing AI to optimize wording, structure, and keyword density.
- Tailoring resumes for specific job descriptions to pass Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS).

### 2. Job Tracker
- Visual Kanban-style board to track applications across different platforms.
- Automated email parsing to log recruiter replies, interviews, and status changes.

### 3. Calendar Scheduler
- Direct integrations with Google Calendar, Outlook, and Apple Calendar.
- Intelligent scheduling that coordinates interview slots, preparation time, and post-interview follow-ups.

### 4. Interview Coach
- Real-time or simulated voice/text AI interview sessions tailored to specific roles and companies.
- Comprehensive feedback on response quality, structure, tone, and delivery.

### 5. Live Translation & Caption Mode
- Fully compliant, consent-based audio translation and live captioning for cross-border interviews and meetings.
- Real-time speech-to-text transcripts for review.

### 6. AI Assistant
- Conversational chat interface for career advice, cover letter writing, salary negotiation tips, and resume critiques.
