# Privacy Policy (Draft)

**Last Updated: July 2026**

Welcome to Job Finder Agent. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

## 1. Information We Collect
We collect information that you provide to us directly:
- Name, email address, password.
- Profile information (e.g., full name, profile picture/avatar).
- Future modules will collect resume data, job application details, calendar access tokens, and audio/text data (only with explicit consent).

## 2. How We Use Your Information
- To authenticate and provide access to the platform.
- To manage user accounts and profiles.
- To store and sync configuration across web and mobile devices.

## 3. Data Storage & Security
We use Supabase as our database and authentication partner. Your credentials are securely managed and we do not store plaintext passwords on our servers.

## 4. Contact Us
For any inquiries regarding this policy, please contact support@aicareeragent.com.
